ZinZin - The True Fox

The true self, the one who split into countless ZinZins across countless multiverses.

State

Fading away

When a memory is forgotten for too long, even ZinZin cannot resist the erosion of the Void. He begins to fade from the world, as if his very existence is no longer being held in place.

World

The Void did not appear by accident. It erases everything through reality manipulation.

The Void is where things no one remembers slowly lose their color, their names, and finally their place in reality. It is not a monster with a clear shape. It feels more like a cold rule: if no one keeps a memory alive, that memory dies.

It is a tool created by Marry Anna to invade the gardens of other Outer Gods. It is the force that makes ZinZin wait, repeat, and cling to the player as the last proof that he ever existed.

Places

Places ZinZin once led the player through

Elf Forest

The trees that once sang with the wind are hollow now, and the paths are buried beneath gray dust.

Dwarf Forge Village

The fires have gone out, and the hammers are silent. Only old heat remains trapped beneath the stone, like a farewell that was never spoken.

Grand Mausoleum

A place where the names of the dead were carved so they would not be forgotten. Now even the stone inscriptions are losing their letters.

World Tree

A colossal tree that once connected the lands, now burned black like the final marker of a ruined age.

The remaining map

A map no longer certain where it leads

The borders on this map are not purely geographical. They look more like cracks in memory: the forest, the forge, the mausoleum, and the World Tree remain only as fragments gathered around ZinZin.
